Introducing ExploreLocal: My First Figma Project and UX Design

Your Ultimate Event Finder App

As I embarked on my journey to become a front-end developer, I took a transformative leap by completing the renowned Google UX Design course on Coursera. As part of this immersive training, I undertook a hands-on project using Figma. In this project, I created an intuitive app with a clear purpose: to empower individuals in their quest for nearby and upcoming events. The idea was that users can effortlessly explore a vibrant array of cultural experiences right in their vicinity. Gone are the days of navigating external websites; this app allows users to conveniently search for events and book their tickets directly within the platform.

Crafted with a user-centric approach, I employed UX design principles to ensure an engaging and seamless experience. This project showcases my ability to transform ideas into tangible, user-centered interfaces and demonstrates my commitment to continuously honing my front-end development skills.

Unleashing Creativity: From Hand-drawn Ideas to Figma Wireframes

In each case of my work, I embark on an exhilarating journey that begins with the stroke of a pen on paper. The initial stage always involves sketching ideas by hand, allowing me to explore and visualize concepts in their rawest form. Once satisfied with the result, I transition to the next step: crafting the first draft of the project.

For this particular project I transformed my drawings into wireframes by the use of figma, but in the other projects I go directly into visual studio code and just start writing. Witnessing the evolution of the project at each stage is a thrilling experience, and I relish the way it transforms and adapts along the way. From the organic strokes of my initial sketches to the precise lines and elements of my Figma wireframes, and then to the code, I bring my ideas to fruition with a meticulous eye for detail. Each step of the process is infused with passion and dedication, as I trive to create captivating and user-centered designs.

Personalized Event Discovery and Booking

The core concept of the App revolves around providing users with a seamless sorting experience. Through intuitive filters and selection criteria, clients can easily specify their preferred event date, desired area, and the type of event they are interested in. This combination of filters ensures that users can efficiently navigate through a wide range of events, saving valuable time and effort.

The App offers a user-friendly interface, prioritizing functionality and ease of use. With clear navigation and intuitive controls, users can quickly access and browse through the event listings, effortlessly narrowing down their options.

All in One

Event booking is streamlined within the app, eliminating the need for external website redirects. The App enables secure and convenient ticket purchases, complete with detailed event information. Users can manage their bookings efficiently, receive event reminders, and access additional event details, ensuring a seamless and organized experience.

Enhancing User Experience in Event Booking

A result of meticulous research conducted during its initial stages, addresses a crucial usability issue that users encountered during event booking. One of the key pain points identified was user confusion regarding the purchase process. Users expected that upon clicking "buy," the app would redirect them to a confirmation page where they could review and accept the payment. However, the low-fidelity prototype failed to incorporate this intermediate step, resulting in immediate purchases being made without confirmation.

Through user feedback and analysis, the interface was carefully adjusted to align with users' expectations. The new design now incorporates a confirmation page, providing users with a clear and explicit opportunity to review their purchase before proceeding with the payment.

A Personalized Event Management App

The App is a robust and highly customizable app that offers a strictly personal experience to users. Users have the ability to create their profiles and set their preferences, enabling them to receive tailored event notifications based on their interests. This personalized approach ensures that users stay informed about events that align with their preferences, enhancing their overall event discovery experience.

One of the key features of EventConnect is its historical tracking capability. Users can easily retrieve their past events, allowing them to revisit their event history and access valuable information about their previous experiences. This feature not only serves as a convenient reference but also enables users to reflect on their event attendance and make informed decisions for future engagements.
